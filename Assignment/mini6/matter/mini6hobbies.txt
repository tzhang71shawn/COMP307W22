            <p>
The essence of buddha's teaching is the four noble truth.<br>
<ol>
  <li>the truth of suffering</li>
  <li>the truth of the cause of suffering</li>
  <li>the truth of the end of suffering</li>
 <li> the truth of the path that leads to the end of suffering</li>
</ol>
In a word, it means suffering exists; it has a cause; it has an end; and it has a cause to bring about its end. In this context, suffering refers to any things that goes against you wills. Since human beings have unlimited desire while living in a limited-matters world, there is always suffering.
